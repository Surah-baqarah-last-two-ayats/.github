# Surah Baqarah Last Two Ayats (Ayat 285–286): Arabic, Translation, Benefits & Explanation

The last two verses of **Surah Al-Baqarah** (آخری دو آیات) hold immense spiritual power, deep meaning, and countless blessings. These ayats are among the most recited verses in Islam and are a source of protection, forgiveness, and mercy.

Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) emphasized their importance and encouraged every Muslim to recite them regularly—especially at night.

---

## 📖 Arabic Text of [Last Two Ayats (2:285–286)](https://duasurah.com/surah-baqarah-ki-last-2-ayat-with-urdu-translation/) 

### Ayat 285
آمَنَ الرَّسُولُ بِمَا أُنزِلَ إِلَيْهِ مِن رَّبِّهِ وَالْمُؤْمِنُونَ ۚ كُلٌّ آمَنَ بِاللَّهِ وَمَلَائِكَتِهِ وَكُتُبِهِ وَرُسُلِهِ لَا نُفَرِّقُ بَيْنَ أَحَدٍ مِّن رُّسُلِهِ ۚ وَقَالُوا سَمِعْنَا وَأَطَعْنَا ۖ غُفْرَانَكَ رَبَّنَا وَإِلَيْكَ الْمَصِيرُ

### Ayat 286
لَا يُكَلِّفُ اللَّهُ نَفْسًا إِلَّا وُسْعَهَا ۚ لَهَا مَا كَسَبَتْ وَعَلَيْهَا مَا اكْتَسَبَتْ ۗ رَبَّنَا لَا تُؤَاخِذْنَا إِن نَّسِينَا أَوْ أَخْطَأْنَا ۚ رَبَّنَا وَلَا تَحْمِلْ عَلَيْنَا إِصْرًا كَمَا حَمَلْتَهُ عَلَى الَّذِينَ مِن قَبْلِنَا ۚ رَبَّنَا وَلَا تُحَمِّلْنَا مَا لَا طَاقَةَ لَنَا بِهِ ۖ وَاعْفُ عَنَّا وَاغْفِرْ لَنَا وَارْحَمْنَا ۚ أَنتَ مَوْلَانَا فَانصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ الْكَافِرِينَ

## 🌍 English Translation

### Ayat 285
> The Messenger has believed in what was revealed to him from his Lord, and so have the believers. All of them believe in Allah, His angels, His books, and His messengers, saying: *“We make no distinction between any of His messengers.”* And they say, *“We hear and we obey. Grant us Your forgiveness, our Lord. To You is the final return.”*

### Ayat 286
> Allah does not burden a soul beyond its capacity. It will have the reward of what it earns and bear the burden of what it commits.  
> *Our Lord, do not hold us accountable if we forget or make a mistake.*  
> *Our Lord, do not place upon us a burden like that which You placed upon those before us.*  
> *Our Lord, do not burden us with what we cannot bear.*  
> *Pardon us, forgive us, and have mercy on us.*  
> *You are our Protector, so help us against the disbelieving people.*

## 🧠 Simple Explanation (Tafsir Summary)

[These two ayats beautifully summarize](https://currentislam.com/surah-baqarah-last-2-ayat/) the **core beliefs of Islam** and the relationship between a believer and Allah.

### 1. Declaration of Faith
- Muslims believe in:
  - Allah
  - Angels
  - Divine Books
  - All Prophets (without discrimination)

### 2. Obedience and Submission
- “We hear and we obey” reflects complete submission to Allah’s commands.

### 3. Allah’s Mercy
- Allah does not burden anyone beyond their ability.
- Islam is a religion of ease, not hardship.

### 4. Powerful Duas Included
These ayats contain multiple important duas:
- Forgiveness for mistakes
- Protection from heavy burdens
- Mercy and help from Allah

- Evil
- Shaytan
- Harm

## ❓ FAQs

### Q1: Which ayats are the last two of Surah Baqarah?  
They are Ayat **285 and 286**.

### Q2: What happens if you recite them every night?  
You receive protection, peace, and sufficient blessings for the night.

### Q3: Are these ayats a dua?  
Yes, especially Ayat 286 contains multiple powerful duas.

### Q4: Can we recite them without wudu?  
Yes, you can recite them from memory without wudu, but wudu is preferred.
